Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22/07/2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Toilet Roll Trouble ("the app", "we", "us") handles information when you use the app.

Information we collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app does not require an account, and does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party trackers.

Data stored on your device

The app saves your best score, unlocked skins, and settings (sound, music, haptics, motion) locally on your device using standard local storage. This data never leaves your device and is removed when you uninstall the app.

Children's privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Third-party services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics providers.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we'll change the "Last updated" date above and post the new version at this URL.
