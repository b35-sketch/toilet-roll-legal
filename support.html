Support

Thanks for playing Toilet Roll Trouble! Here's how to get help.

How to play

  • Hold the screen to tighten the roll and duck under high hazards.
  • Release to loosen and jump over floor hazards.
  • Grab golden paper to score bonuses. Don't fall in the hole!

Common questions

No sound? Check your device's silent switch and that Sound/Music are enabled in the game's Settings screen.

Progress lost? Scores and unlocks are stored locally on your device. Reinstalling the app will reset them.

Game feels laggy? Try enabling "Reduced motion" in Settings.
